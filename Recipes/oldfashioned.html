Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Rye or Bourbon Whiskey
  • 1 Dash of Angostura Bitters
  • 1 Sugar Cube
  • Splash of Club Soda
  • 1 Cherry & Orange Twist

Directions

Stir Whiskey, bitters, and sugar cube well with ice in a mixing glass, then strain over ice into a rocks glass. Once poured into your glass, add a splash of water or club soda and garnish with a cherry and orange twist.
